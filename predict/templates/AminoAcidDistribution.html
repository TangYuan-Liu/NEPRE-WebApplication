Amino Acid Pairs Distribution

Introduction

Each kind amino acid has own ditribution preference. We counted the existing PDB dataset(June 2017) and analyzed the distribution of each kind of amino acid pairs.

For each kind of amino acid pair, we establish a spherical coordinate and split them as fllows,
