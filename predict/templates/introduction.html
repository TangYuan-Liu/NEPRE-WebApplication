Introduction

Background

Nepre(Scoring Function for Protein Structure Assessment Based on Neighbourhood Preference) provide a efficient, portable and flexible python module to use. By using the online server, you can finish the calculating job with a single browser.

There are many methods for predicting the three-dimensional structure of proteins, mainly based on physical and chemical principles, statistical principles, and biological principles. Nepre establishes the algorithm by using a statistical result of the Protein Data Bank.

Nepre provide two sub-algorithm for evaluating the structure, Nepre-R and Nepre-F.

Nepre-R uses the statistical result of radius of each kind amino acid as the distance paramter, amino pairs which the distance lower than the sum of radius will be regard as the effective pair. The radius is the gaussian mean value of the statistical results.

Nepre-F uses a relatively universal distance parameter, 6 angstrom, as the distance cutoff. Amino pairs which the distance lower than 6 angstrom will be regard as the effective pair.

Organization

The Nepre WebServer is organized as follows,

Method will introduce the detailed analysis of the algorithm.

Protein Structure Predict will provide the online application for predicting protein tertiary struture.

Amino Acid Distribution will display the amino acid pairs distribution tendency.

History will provide a service for users to check their history results.

Download will provide some doucuments and our offline system download service.
